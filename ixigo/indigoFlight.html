IndiGo Connectivity and Fleet Information

IndiGo, the flagship carrier of India, boasts a robust network that spans both domestic and international destinations. With over 100 destinations worldwide and an impressive fleet, Air India connects major Indian cities to global hubs.

Fleet Information:

  • Airbus A320-200 and A321neo: These aircraft form the backbone of Indigo's fleet, known for their fuel efficiency and passenger comfort.
  • ATR 72-600: Used for regional flights connecting smaller cities, providing essential connectivity to remote areas.

Indigo operates over 2000 flights daily, ensuring widespread connectivity and frequent services on popular routes.

Baggage Allowance Policy of IndiGo

Cabin Baggage:

  • All Class:All Classes: Passengers can carry one cabin bag weighing up to 7 kg and 115 cms (L+W+H), along with one personal item (like a laptop bag or handbag).

Checked Baggage:

Domestic Flights:

  • Standard: Up to 15 kg. Passengers can pre-book extra baggage allowance if needed.

International Flights:

  • Varies based on the destination, generally up to 20-30 kg depending on the route and fare type.

Note: Always check your ticket details for specific allowances as they can vary by fare type and route.Know more about Indigo baggage allowance.

Services Provided by IndiGo

  • Pre-booked Meals: Passengers can pre-book meals and snacks from a selection of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options.
  • IndiGo Early: Allows passengers to reach their destination ahead of schedule on an earlier flight if seats are available.
  • IndiGo Promise:An insurance policy to cover delays, loss of baggage, and flight cancellations.
  • 6E Prime:A bundle offering seat selection, meal, and priority check-in.

IndiGo Refund and Cancellation Policies

IndiGo's refund and cancellation policies are designed to offer flexibility and convenience to passengers, though specific terms and conditions can vary based on the fare class and route.

Cancellation Policies:

Domestic Flights:

  • Economy:A cancellation fee is applicable, and the remaining amount is refunded.
  • Executive/Business Class:Cancellation fees are generally lower, and the balance is refunded.

International Flights:

  • Economy and Business Class:Cancellation fees apply, and the terms can vary based on the fare category and route.

Refund Process:

  • Cancellations and refund requests can be initiated through the IndiGo website, mobile app, or contact customer service.
  • Provide the necessary booking details and identification.
  • Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days of the original mode of payment.

Note: Non-refundable tickets may not be eligible for a refund but could be eligible for travel credit. Always check the fare rules at the time of booking.

IndiGo Travel Documents

Traveling with IndiGo involves understanding and preparing necessary travel documents to ensure a smooth and hassle-free journey. Proper documentation is crucial whether traveling domestically or internationally. Here's a comprehensive guide to the travel documents required by Air India:

Photo Identification Proof

A government-issued photo ID is mandatory. Acceptable IDs include:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID
  • Passport
  • Driving License
  • PAN Card
  • Government/Defense Photo ID
  • School/College ID (for students)

E-ticket or Paper Ticket

A printed copy of the e-ticket or the paper ticket should be carried for verification at check-in and boarding.

Boarding Pass

After check-in, either online or at the airport, passengers need to ensure they have their boarding pass ready.

Special Documents

  • For infants and children, a birth certificate or school ID may be required.
  • Senior citizens should carry proof of age to avail of discounts.
  • Armed forces personnel should carry valid service IDs and relevant travel authorization, if applicable.

Check IndiGo travel document requirements before traveling.
